For the spreadsheet gui, I had to implement a way of inserting values into cells by use of a formula textbox

The only way I could get them to enter into the cell was through the use of the enter key or detecting when they clicked another cell

In order to save and open files, I had to use examples from MSDN for SaveFileDialog and OpenFileDialog

For the additional features I added a graphing feature, I used the chart toolbox item that C# already provided
